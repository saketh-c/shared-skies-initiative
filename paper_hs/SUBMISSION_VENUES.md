# Submission venues — conferences & journals (independent HS authors, no mentor)

For: two independent high-school students, no faculty mentor/affiliation. Paper:
applied ML + environmental justice — spatially-honest (leave-one-sensor-out)
PM2.5 prediction in Texas + EJ disparity audit + deployed free public web map.
Research compiled 18 June 2026. **Verify every deadline's timezone + any
remote-presentation option on the official page before relying on it.**

Strategy note: keep a **double-blind (anonymized)** version AND a **non-anonymized**
version ready. "Archival" venues publish your paper (good credential, but commits
the work); "non-archival" workshops don't, so you can submit the same work to
multiple non-archival venues and/or an archival one later.

### Prestige stretch — real academic journals (no affiliation gate; APC waivable)
- **Environmental Data Science (Cambridge University Press)** ⭐ — rolling; **ISSN 2634-4602, Scopus + DOAJ indexed**, transparent peer review. Scope explicitly includes ML for environment. *The* prestigious home for the actual science — high bar, writing must be fully professional. https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/environmental-data-science/information/author-instructions/preparing-your-materials · OA/waiver https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/environmental-data-science/information/journal-policies/open-access-options
- **Climate Informatics conference** → papers publish in EDS. CI 2026 passed (Jan 16, 2026); **CI 2027 not yet announced** (~Jan 2027 expected). http://www.climateinformatics.org/conferences/

### Lower-tier journals (legit but fees / lighter review)
- **Journal of High School Science (JHSS)** — ISSN 2575-6206, mentor-optional, single-blind; fee unclear (~$85 reported vs "free") — confirm. https://jhss.scholasticahq.com/for-authors
- **International Journal of High School Research (IJHSR)** — $250 on acceptance; **EBSCO-indexed** (a real plus). https://ijhsr.terrajournals.org/
- **Curieux Academic Journal** — $200–250 on acceptance; lighter review. https://www.curieuxacademicjournal.com/submissions

### Avoid (for your situation)
- **JEI (Journal of Emerging Investigators)** — reputable but **requires a senior mentor as last author** and bars sole student authorship → excluded by your no-mentor constraint.
- **JSR (Journal of Student Research)** — pay-to-publish (~$50 + $299), documented hollow review, not in Scopus/WoS/DOAJ. Skip.
- **AAQR / MDPI (Sustainability, Atmosphere, IJERPH)** — credible/indexed but ~$2,000–2,900 APCs with no student waiver → cost-prohibitive.

## Preprint / citable DOI (NOT peer review — for a public, timestamped record)
- **Zenodo** (https://zenodo.org) — instant DOI, no affiliation; archive the model + Texas PM2.5 dataset + a code snapshot, each with its own DOI.
- **OSF Preprints** (https://osf.io) — free, auto-DOI, no affiliation; good for the paper preprint.
- **arXiv** — now hard: as of Jan 21, 2026 an unaffiliated first-time cs.LG submitter needs a **personal endorsement** from an established arXiv author. Default to Zenodo/OSF.
